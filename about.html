About the Organisers

Jones Day

Jones Day is a global law firm with more than 2,500 lawyers in 42 offices across five continents. The Firm is distinguished by a singular tradition of client service; the mutual commitment to, and the seamless collaboration of, a true partnership; formidable legal talent across multiple disciplines and jurisdictions; and shared professional values that focus on client needs.

Centre for Commercial Law in Asia (CCLA), SMU Yong Pung How School of Law

The Centre for Commercial Law in Asia (CCLA) was established in 2014 by the Singapore Management University’s (SMU) Yong Pung How School of Law (YPHSL). The CCLA is a research centre that aims to be Singapore’s and Asia’s leading centre for research, education, and thought leadership in Commercial Law. Its mission is to contribute to the development and understanding of Commercial Law through research, publications, and events.

EW Barker Centre for Law & Business, NUS Faculty of Law

The EW Barker Centre for Law & Business at the National University of Singapore (NUS) Faculty of Law is the successor to the former Centre for Commercial Law Studies (CCLS). The Centre promotes interdisciplinary research and education at the intersection of law and business. It organises conferences, workshops, and public lectures on corporate law, financial regulation, and commercial law topics.
