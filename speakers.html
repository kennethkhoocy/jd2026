Speakers

Professor Lucian A. Bebchuk
James Barr Ames Professor of Law, Economics, and Finance, and Director of the Program on Corporate Governance, Harvard Law School
He is a leading scholar in corporate governance, corporate finance, and law and economics. His research has been published in top academic journals. He is a Research Associate at the National Bureau of Economic Research, a Fellow of the European Corporate Governance Institute, and a past editor of the Review of Financial Studies. He has testified before Congress and served as an adviser to regulatory bodies.
Professor Dan W. Puchniak
Professor, SMU Yong Pung How School of Law; Director, Centre for Commercial Law in Asia (CCLA)
He is an internationally recognized scholar of comparative corporate law and governance with a focus on Asia. His research challenges Western assumptions about corporate governance and explores how Asian corporate governance systems function in practice. He has published extensively in leading law journals.
TH
Professor Tjio Hans
CJ Koh Professor of Law, NUS Faculty of Law
He specializes in securities regulation, corporate law, and financial regulation. He is a well-known authority on Singapore securities law and has authored several leading texts in the field. He has served on various regulatory advisory committees.
